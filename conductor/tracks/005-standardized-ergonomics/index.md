# Track 005: Standardized Ergonomic Assessments

## Objective
Implement validated ergonomic scoring frameworks (RULA/REBA) to provide industry-standard risk assessments for office and standing desk environments.

## Scope
- RULA (Rapid Upper Limb Assessment) implementation.
- REBA (Rapid Entire Body Assessment) for standing modes.
- Automated ergonomic report generation.
