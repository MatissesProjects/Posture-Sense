# Track 017: Fatigue Micro-Movement Detection

## Objective
Detect early-onset fatigue by analyzing subtle postural shifts and micro-movements (fidgeting) that precede visible slouching.

## Scope
- Frequency and amplitude analysis of pose jitter.
- Weight-shifting (lateral/sagittal) detection.
- Correlation between micro-movements and subsequent posture degradation.
- Predictive "Pre-Fatigue" alerts.
