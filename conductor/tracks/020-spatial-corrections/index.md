# Track 020: Asymmetrical Strain & Spatial Audio

## Objective
Identify and correct asymmetrical postural habits (e.g., leaning to one side) using directional feedback.

## Scope
- Lateral spinal deviation (leaning) tracking.
- Asymmetrical shoulder load analysis.
- Spatial audio "nudges" (3D sound) to guide users back to the center.
