# Track 012: Biomechanical Modeling

## Objective
Develop a high-fidelity 3D biomechanical model of the user to analyze balance, shear forces on the spine, and overall skeletal alignment.

## Scope
- 3D Coordinate Normalization.
- Center of Mass (CoM) estimation.
- Kinematic Chain Analysis (Lumbar to Cervical).
- Gravity-axis alignment.
