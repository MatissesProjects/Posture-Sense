# Track 011: Hand & Wrist Ergonomics

## Objective
Monitor hand posture and wrist alignment during keyboard and mouse usage to prevent repetitive strain injuries (RSI).

## Scope
- MediaPipe Hands integration.
- Ulnar/Radial deviation detection.
- "Claw Hand" and finger tension analysis.
- Keyboard/Mouse interaction risk scoring.
