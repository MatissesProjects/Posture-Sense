# Track 019: Predictive Sit/Stand Coaching

## Objective
Optimize sit/stand desk usage by predicting when a user's posture is about to degrade and suggesting a transition *before* pain or slouching occurs.

## Scope
- Time-to-Slouch (TTS) modeling for sitting vs. standing.
- Personalized transition schedules.
- Impact analysis of transitions on overall focus and comfort.
