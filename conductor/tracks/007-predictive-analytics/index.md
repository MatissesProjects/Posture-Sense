# Track 007: Predictive Health & Advanced Analytics

## Objective
Analyze long-term posture trends and use predictive modeling to identify musculoskeletal disorder (MSD) risks before they manifest as pain.

## Scope
- Longitudinal posture data storage.
- Predictive models for fatigue and slouching patterns.
- Heatmaps of workstation usage (e.g., monitor gaze distribution).
