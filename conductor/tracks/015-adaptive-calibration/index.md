# Track 015: Adaptive Calibration & Environmental Awareness

## Objective
Ensure the system remains accurate and robust in varying physical environments, lighting conditions, and webcam orientations.

## Scope
- Multi-Monitor Neutral Baselines.
- Ambient Light Audit (Detecting "Squinting" and "Leaning").
- Webcam Angle/Foreshortening Compensation.
- Background noise and multi-person filtering.
