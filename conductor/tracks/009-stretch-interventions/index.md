# Track 009: Smart Stretch & Recovery Interventions

## Objective
Automatically suggest and guide the user through targeted stretches based on detected postural strain and prolonged sedentary behavior.

## Scope
- Stretch Suggestion Engine (logic based on posture duration).
- Guided visual micro-breaks (chin tucks, shoulder rolls, 20-20-20 rule).
- "Tech Neck" recovery specialized for stacked monitor users.
- Stretch compliance tracking.
