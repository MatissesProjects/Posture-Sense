# Track 016: Clinical Ergonomic Metrics

## Objective
Implement formal clinical metrics used by physical therapists to provide a medical-grade assessment of postural health.

## Scope
- Craniovertebral Angle (CVA) tracking.
- Forward Head Posture (FHP) quantification.
- Shoulder protraction/retraction analysis.
- Integration with clinical reporting templates.
