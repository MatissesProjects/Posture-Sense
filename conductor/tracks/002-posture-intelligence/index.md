# Track 002: Posture Intelligence

This track focuses on the "judgment" logic: converting pose coordinates into a meaningful posture score and ergonomic advice.

## Status: *Not Started*

## Goals
- Quantify ergonomic alignment for stacked monitors.
- Create a normalized "Posture Score" (0-100).
- Automatically detect or receive input for sitting vs. standing.
- Provide real-time corrective suggestions.
