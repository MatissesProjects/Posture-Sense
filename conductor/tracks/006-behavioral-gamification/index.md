# Track 006: Behavioral Science & Gamification

## Objective
Use behavioral psychology and gamification to encourage long-term posture improvement and healthy work habits.

## Scope
- Micro-interventions and non-intrusive notifications.
- 20-20-20 rule for eye strain prevention.
- Daily streaks and "Posture Scores" to drive engagement.
