# Track 021: Respiration & Stress Monitoring

## Objective
Estimate the user's breathing rate and detect "Screen Apnea" or high-stress patterns to trigger mindfulness interventions.

## Scope
- Shoulder/Chest vertical displacement tracking.
- Breathing rate estimation (RPM).
- Screen Apnea detection (breath-holding during intense focus).
- Mindfulness-based breathing prompts.
